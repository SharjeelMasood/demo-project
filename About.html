A team of talented and dedicated experts in the fields of e-commerce, digital media, supply chain, logistics, customer services, finance and operations is already onboard to make this venture a resounding success and a case study for other organisations to replicate in order to become successful in Pakistan and beyond.

mycart.pk also provides businesses with an opportunity for organisations to setup corporate accounts to manage purchases of business consumables and non-durable items. Since mycart.pk is a registered and compliant business entity, all purchases made through mycart.pk can be treated as documented purchases under the prevailing tax provisions.

To be an exemplary business and a model corporate citizen in a country faced with numerous challenges, mycart.pk has plans to become a paperless work environment to protect the trees, focus on consumption of clean, renewable energy and maintain a minimal carbon footprint. To make these plans a reality, mycart.pk is already experimenting with a fully electric powered delivery fleet and is in the process of installing solar panels to convert the corporate offices and fulfilment centre to an off-grid facility.
